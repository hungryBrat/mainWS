First skill family: `vagabond` — cross-domain synthesis inspired by the Vagabond Protocol document.

Three composable modes:
- **walk** (default): extracts a topic's "frequency" (core tension/paradox), finds resonant domains, includes one irrational leap as entropy injection. Narrative output. MCMC + Strange Loops + abductive reasoning.

The three are complements, not competitors. Walk finds the terrain, Lattice maps it, Shadow breaks the map.
